Our Guidelines

Respect & Inclusivity

  • Treat everyone with respect, regardless of skill level, background, or identity.
  • No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination will be tolerated.

Professional Collaboration

  • Give feedback that is constructive, specific, and encouraging.
  • Critique ideas, not people. Example: “You could improve this by…” instead of “This is bad.”

Integrity & Authenticity

  • Submit only work that is your own—no plagiarism or misrepresentation.
  • Be transparent about your role and contributions in projects.

Trust & Credibility

  • Give honest, unbiased reviews and endorsements.
  • Do not manipulate ratings, create fake accounts, or misuse the platform.

Safety & Security

  • Do not share harmful or malicious content.
  • Respect others’ privacy and protect your own account.

Moderation & Enforcement

  • Violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or bans.
  • All moderation decisions are based on these guidelines to ensure fairness.
